Where is Dare Dare ?

Pacific Cup tracker

The best way to track our position and that of our competition during the race is on the race tracker.

Direct link: https://pacificcup.org/tracking.html

Dare Dare Garmin tracker

This tracker is a backup device we will be using during the race. We will configure it to update our location every 4 hours. This method might fail if we forget to charge the battery for the tracker so do not worry if it does not update!

Direct link: http://share.garmin.com/daredare
